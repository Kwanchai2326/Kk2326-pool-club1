How to Play

  • Drag mouse/touch on table screen to rotate and aim your cue stick.
  • Pull back the cue stick or watch the left Power Meter to set strike force.
  • Release the drag to hit the cue ball.
  • Sinking any ball extends your timer! Sinking the cue ball (scratch) penalizes time.
  • Clear all 15 object balls to win the shootout!
